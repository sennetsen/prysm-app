About Us

About Us

Hi! I’m Caroline—a Cornell student, lifelong artist, and the founder of Prysm. I’ve been drawing since I was four and started sharing my work online in middle school.

I built Prysm with my friends (shout-out Sennet, Nikhil, and Maggie!) because we wanted to reimagine how creators and communities connect: not just through content, but through real collaboration.

I believe our creations are what make life worth living, and that creators deserve tools that recognize the value they bring to the world.

Thanks for being part of Prysm 💖

~ Caroline Zhu
